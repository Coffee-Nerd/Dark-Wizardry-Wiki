# Dispel

Syntax: cast 'dispel magic' \<target\>  

This spell attempts to remove magic affects from a given target.  It  
may be used as an offensive spell to remove an enemy's magical advantages,  
or as a benign spell to remove a friend's (or one's own) magical handicaps.
