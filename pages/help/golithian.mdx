# Golithian

The Golithian is a race not disimilar to the Tyromp - tall, strong,  
intelligent and calculating, the Golithian are humanoid - if not everything  
that the average human would aspire to be. They stand tall at two to three  
meters and have extremely muscular bodies. They devote themselves well to  
the magical arts as well, exceling with offensive magical spells.

            +Magic Damage:            remort level + 1%
