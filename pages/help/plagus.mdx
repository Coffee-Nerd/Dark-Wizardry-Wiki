# Plagus

The Plagus: a vile creature - somewhere inbetween a gargoyle and  
an imp - extremely powerful and concentrated in the offensive magical  
arts, given them superior hitting power with their spells. The Plagus  
also resist magic well, regain mana quickly and can fly when the need  
arises. They can move quickly and think faster.  

Bonuses:  
            +Magic Damage:            remort level + 1%  
            Faster Mana Regeneration  
            Resist Magic  
            Flying  

Base Stats:         Str 13, Int 19, Wis 19, Dex 16, Con 13  
Max After Training: Str 18, Int 24, Wis 24, Dex 21, Con 18  
Max With Equipment: Str 22, Int 28, Wis 28, Dex 25, Con 22
