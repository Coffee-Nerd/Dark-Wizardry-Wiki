# Inlay

Syntax: inlay set    \<gemstone\> \<weapon/armor\>  
Syntax: inlay remove \<gemstone\> \<weapon/armor\>  
Syntax: inlay stats  \<gemstone\>  
Syntax: inlay check  \<object\>  
Syntax: inlay list  

INLAY SET will set a gemstone into a weapon or piece of armor.  
INLAY REMOVE will remove a gemstone from whatever it is set into.  
INLAY CHECK will check the gemstone slots and current gemstones of an item.  
INLAY STATS will give detailed stats on a gemstone.  
INLAY LIST will list all your gemstones.  

Setting Gemstones into your weapons or armor will enhance the abilities  
of the item.
