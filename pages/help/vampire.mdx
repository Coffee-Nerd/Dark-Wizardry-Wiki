  Vampires are supernatural mutations of a human. Having enhanced abilities  
in the night, such as the ability to drain an opponents life force.  Vampires  
are generally intelligent, fairly agile, wise, and strong, but do not take  
hits very well. Vampires lend themselves well to the magical arts and can  
BITE un-expectant victims to drain their life-force. They hate the day and  
are generally can't perform as well, affecting even their core attributes.  
In addition they are unable to regenerate or drink from a victim. At night  
they come into their own and regenerate faster than many other races.  

Bonuses:  
            +Magic Damage:            remort level + 1% (Night Only)  
            Faster Mana Regeneration  
            Vampire's Bite (saps health and mana from un-expectant foe)  

Base Stats:         Str 16, Int 18, Wis 16, Dex 15, Con 12  
Max After Training: Str 21, Int 23, Wis 21, Dex 20, Con 17  
Max With Equipment: Str 25, Int 27, Wis 25, Dex 24, Con 21 (-1 in the day)
