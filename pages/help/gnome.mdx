# Gnome

Gnomes are a small race of wirey, but tough creatures.  They  
are stronger than halflings but not quite as sneaky.  They  
naturally see in the dark, and therefore make good thieves,  
able to see where others can not.  They're decent at most classes.  

Base Stats:         Str 12, Int 13, Wis 14, Dex 13, Con 13  
Max After Training: Str 17, Int 18, Wis 19, Dex 18, Con 18  
Max With Equipment: Str 21, Int 22, Wis 23, Dex 22, Con 22
