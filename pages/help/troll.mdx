  Trolls are 6-9 feet tall with brownish or greenish scaley skin.  
They are natural rangers due to their massive strength and size.  
They make great tanks, like giants, but are also decent in other  
classes.  They regenerate fast, but find it hard to sneak around  
among the small folk.  

Base Stats:         Str 15, Int 12, Wis 11, Dex 12, Con 15  
Max After Training: Str 20, Int 17, Wis 16, Dex 17, Con 20  
Max With Equipment: Str 24, Int 21, Wis 20, Dex 21, Con 24
