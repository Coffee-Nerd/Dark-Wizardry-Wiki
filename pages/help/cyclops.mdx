# Cyclops

Cyclopses are towering creatures with just one eye. Not the smartest  
but exceptionally strong, the cyclops is a truely terrifying creature  
to behold. Cyclopses heal faster than many other races, but are unable  
to sneak or hide.  

Base Stats:         Str 17, Int 10, Wis 12, Dex 13, Con 18  
Max After Training: Str 22, Int 15, Wis 17, Dex 18, Con 23  
Max With Equipment: Str 26, Int 20, Wis 21, Dex 22, Con 26
