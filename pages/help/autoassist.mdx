# Autoassist

Syntax: autoassist  
Syntax: assist \<character\>  

AUTOASSIST toggles your ability to automatically assist group members in a  
fight.  

ASSIST will make you assist a character.  The character must be in your group  
to succeed.  

See CONFIGURE to find out which toggle is on and which is off.
