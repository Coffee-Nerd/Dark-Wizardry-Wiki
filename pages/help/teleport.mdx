# Teleport

Syntax: cast \<teleport\>  

This spell takes you from your current location to a random location somewhere  
in the world.  

BEWARE, USING THIS SPELL WILL ALMOST ALWAYS END UP WITH YOU DYING TO SOME CRAZY MOB,  
WITHOUT YOU KNOWING HOW TO GET BACK. USE AT YOUR OWN RISK.
