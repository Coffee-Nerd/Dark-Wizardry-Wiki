# Wythian

The Wythian are a people of the shadow. Tall and lithe, not unlike a vampire  
in appearance, they are essentially a race of assassins. Extremely quick, the  
Wythian gain no benefit from the haste spell (and can QUICKEN when slowed, even  
in combat), they can detect hidden and resist poison.  

Bonuses:  
            Racial Haste  
            Detect Hidden  
            Resist Poison  

Base Stats:         Str 16, Int 13, Wis 14, Dex 20, Con 17  
Max After Training: Str 21, Int 18, Wis 19, Dex 25, Con 22  
Max With Equipment: Str 25, Int 22, Wis 23, Dex 29, Con 26
