# Alert

Syntax: alert  

This skill improves your awareness. It makes you see sneaking and tracking  
characters, and it is HARD to succesfully backstab/impale an alert character.  
However, characters with ENHANCED BACKSTAB or SWORD EXPERTNESS won't have  
trouble attacking alert characters.
