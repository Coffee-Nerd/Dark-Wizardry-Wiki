# Ogre

The huge lumbing Ogre is strong and thick skinned. They are smarter  
than your average Cyclops but not quite as strong. They natrually heal  
faster than other races, but are unable to sneak or hide.  

Base Stats:         Str 16, Int 13, Wis 12, Dex 13, Con 16  
Max After Training: Str 21, Int 18, Wis 17, Dex 18, Con 21  
Max With Equipment: Str 25, Int 22, Wis 21, Dex 22, Con 25
