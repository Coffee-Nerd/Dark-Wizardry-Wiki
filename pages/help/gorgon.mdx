# Gorgon

The gorgon are directly descended from Medusa.  From birth, they can  
instantly kill any being which is foolish enough to look them in the  
eye.  Gorgon are very nimble fighters, with great physical prowess.  
Only the foolhardy or the suicidal would dare cross a gorgon.
