# Tomte

Similar to a gnome, the Tomte are short. They are wise and reasonably  
strong, capable of most tasks. Tomte can see in the dark and can always  
furrow out hidden objects or people.  

Base Stats:         Str 13, Int 14, Wis 15, Dex 14, Con 14  
Max After Training: Str 18, Int 19, Wis 20, Dex 19, Con 19  
Max With Equipment: Str 22, Int 23, Wis 24, Dex 23, Con 23
