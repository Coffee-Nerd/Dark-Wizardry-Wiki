  Angels are celestial beings, which take the forms of winged humans of incredible  
beauty. Angels do not have any special prowess in combat but they do have holy  
protection against those of the dark orders, and can fly at will. In the day, the  
light of the Lord imbues them with magical power, enhancing their spellcasting  
prowess.
