# Eldar

While most of the Elves that dwell in the East of Middle Earth are dark elves,  
there are some yet dwelling under the reign of their kings, Elured and Elurin,  
whose realm is in the Hidden City  

Base Stats:         Str 13, Int 16, Wis 17, Dex 15, Con 13  
Max After Training: Str 18, Int 21, Wis 18, Dex 20, Con 18  
Max With Equipment: Str 22, Int 25, Wis 22, Dex 24, Con 22
