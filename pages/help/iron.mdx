# Iron

IRON HAND will make your hand of iron, but only if you DON'T wear a weapon.  
Use of skill is automatic once practiced.  

TALON RIP has the chance to temporarily stun your victim.  It will only work  
when you using IRON HAND.  So when you have IRON HAND practiced and not using  
a weapon.  Use of skill is automatic once practiced.
