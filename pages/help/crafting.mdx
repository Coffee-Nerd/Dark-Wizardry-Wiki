# Crafting

Crafting is still currently under construction, but will be a brand  
new system to craft tools and armor sets!  This will be an alternative to  
killing stuff and finding loot, and will also provide XP and let you create  
unique items with materials you find, harvest, gather, or mine from around  
the world!
