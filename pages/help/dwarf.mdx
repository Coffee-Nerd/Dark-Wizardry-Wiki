  Dwarves are short, stocky fellows, easily identified by their  
size and shape.  They average 3 to 4 feet tall, but are very  
strong, making them good fighters.  Dwarves are born with the  
ability to see in the dark, and have a natural resistance to  
poison.  

Base Stats:         Str 15, Int 12, Wis 13, Dex 12, Con 14  
Max After Training: Str 20, Int 17, Wis 18, Dex 17, Con 19  
Max With Equipment: Str 24, Int 21, Wis 22, Dex 21, Con 23
