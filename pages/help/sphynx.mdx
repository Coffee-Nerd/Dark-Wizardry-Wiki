# Sphynx

The mighty Sphynx, half feline and half avian, can soar majestically  
through the clouds or leap through the city streets with equal ease.  
Highly dextrous, they can dodge without any trouble from birth, and  
with a cat-like temprement they can be flung easily into a berserk-  
rage.  

Base Stats:         Str 16, Int 13, Wis 13, Dex 13, Con 15  
Max After Training: Str 21, Int 18, Wis 18, Dex 18, Con 20  
Max With Equipment: Str 25, Int 22, Wis 22, Dex 22, Con 24
