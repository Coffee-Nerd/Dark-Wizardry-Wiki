# Suicidal

Syntax: cast 'suicidal attack' \<value\>  

This spell will improve your hit and damroll by 'value'.  Though, due to  
the power of this spell, hitpoints will be drained from the caster.  The  
higher the value, the more hitpoints it will cost.
