# Tallfellow

Tallfellows are somewhat rare among halfling folk. Tallfellows are 4 feet tall  
or more and weigh between 30 and 35 pounds. They generally speak Elven in  
addition to Common and Halfling.  

Base Stats:         Str 12, Int 14, Wis 14, Dex 16, Con 14  
Max After Training: Str 17, Int 19, Wis 19, Dex 21, Con 19  
Max With Equipment: Str 22, Int 22, Wis 23, Dex 26, Con 22
