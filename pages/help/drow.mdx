# Drow

Drow cause a lot of chaos wherever they go.  They fled from  
the underground to escape the wrath.  Accustomed to the  
darkness, drow don't always like light very much.  Drow use  
their infravision like gnomes as an advantage over their foes.  
Drows make great mages and thieves.  

Base Stats:         Str 12, Int 14, Wis 13, Dex 14, Con 12  
Max After Training: Str 17, Int 19, Wis 18, Dex 19, Con 17  
Max With Equipment: Str 21, Int 23, Wis 22, Dex 23, Con 21
