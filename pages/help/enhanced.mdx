# Enhanced

DAMAGE: This skill increases the damage you inflict when attacking.  
BASH: This skill increases the damage you inflict when attacking.  

ENHANCED BACKSTAB also improves backstab damage, but a person with enhanced  
backstab can also backstab ALERT characters.  

Use of the skills are automatic once you have practiced it.
