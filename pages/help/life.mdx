# Life

Syntax: cast 'life drain' \<target\>  
Syntax: cast 'soul drain' \<target\>  

LIFE DRAIN will drain hit points from a victim and gives the drained hit points  
to the caster.  

SOUL DRAIN will drain mana points from a victim and gives the drained mana  
points to the caster.
