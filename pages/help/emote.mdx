# Emote

Syntax: emote \<action\>  
Syntax: pose  

EMOTE is used to express emotions or actions.  Besides EMOTE, there are  
several dozen built-in SOCIAL commands, such as CACKLE, HUG, and THANK.  

POSE is a variant of EMOTE.
