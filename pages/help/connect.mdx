# Connect

Syntax: connect \<item1\> \<item2\>  

This command will allow you to attemt to connect two seperate items together.  
If you are unable to connect them, you will be told so.  Connecting items in  
this realm can lead to great treasures and/or evil foes.  
Be wary of what you do. :)
