# Realspellname

REALSPELLNAME toggles between showing you the flavor text, and the real  
text When you are casting spells.  'saeptum apentus' vs 'armor' for casting  
armor, for example.  Some people do not care to see the flavor text, so they  
can toggle this to see the real skill names.
