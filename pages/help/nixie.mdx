# Nixie

Most nixies are slim and comely, with lightly scaled, pale green skin  
and dark green hair. Females often twine shells and pearl strings in  
their hair and dress in wraps woven from colorful seaweed. Males wear  
loincloths of the same materials. Nixies prefer not to leave their lakes.  

Base Stats:         Str 11, Int 17, Wis 16, Dex 15, Con 11  
Max After Training: Str 16, Int 22, Wis 21, Dex 20, Con 16  
Max With Equipment: Str 20, Int 26, Wis 25, Dex 24, Con 20
